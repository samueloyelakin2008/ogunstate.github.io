About Ogun State

Ogun State has a population of about 2 million people and is one of the 36 states that make up the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The state government is composed of the executive, legislative, and judicial branches, whose powers are vested by the Constitution in the Governor, the House of Assembly, and the High Court.

The Ogun State Agro-Airport

The agro international airport located at Iperu-Ilishan Road in Ijebu Local Government Area is 90% completed. This airport aims to boost the agricultural sector and the economy of Ogun State. It was established by Prince Dapo Abiodun to facilitate agricultural development.

2024 Ogun State Sports Festival

Ogun State will host the 22nd National Sports Festival in 2024. Governor Prince Dapo Abiodun has inaugurated a 16-man Local Organising Committee to ensure the event showcases the talent, culture, and hospitality of the state.
